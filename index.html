LiftChain

Follow The Sound...

LiftChain produces melodic trance, progressive, and other styles of electronic music. His releases on Premier League Recordings and JourneyDeep Records have attracted attention from top DJ’s. His 2020 ‘Metamorphosis’ track earned two plays on Paul Van Dyk’s VONYC Session (#720 & #722), and Jes’ UTB #403. LiftChain continues to evolve with his latest vocal release ‘Dream Aloud’, a powerfully emotional track. LiftChain (aka Daniel Lemmon) lives in the Denver area of Colorado, USA where he has lived most of his life. He can’t wait to share more music with all of you. Thank you for stopping by...
